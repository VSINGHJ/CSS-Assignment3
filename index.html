Reese's Peanut Butter Cups

Reese's Peanut Butter Cups are a popular candy manufactured by The Hershey Company. They consist of a smooth peanut butter filling coated with milk chocolate.

Interesting Facts

  • Reese's Peanut Butter Cups were invented in 1928 by H.B. Reese, a former dairy farmer and shipping foreman for Milton S. Hershey.
  • The original name for Reese's Peanut Butter Cups was "Penny Cups".
  • Reese's Peanut Butter Cups are the best-selling candy brand in the United States.
  • There are various spin-off products based on Reese's Peanut Butter Cups, including Reese's Pieces, Reese's Sticks, and Reese's Big Cup.
